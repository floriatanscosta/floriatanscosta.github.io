Between Falcon tubes and algorithms, a researcher in search of answers! Science is a universe of possibilities. Between tubes, analytical curves, spectrometers, and data, I follow invisible traces that tell stories and reveal the mysteries of nature. Every chemical element has its own signature, and every sample is an enigma to be unraveled. It is in this space between the visible and the hidden that my research takes place, seeking to understand the story of each analyzed sample. Each result is a clue and each experiment, a new chapter in the search for answers. On this site, I share some of these stories, work, and productions.
